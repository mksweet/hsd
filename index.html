What is Head Start?

Head Start is a free child care and pre-school program for kids from birth to 5 years old. We also provide services for the whole family, even pregnant mothers can enroll. Our program is for families who meet certain requirements.

Head Start has well-trained teachers who are devoted to the success of your whole family. 

Some locations offer full-day programs and others offer half-day programs.

Kids get to:

  • develop basic math and reading skills
  • interact with other kids their age
  • learn and have fun at the same time
  • eat healthy breakfast, lunch, and snacks
  • access free health services
  • Parents and guardians get to:
  • access free career training and job opportunities
  • meet other families with young children
  • learn about child development
  • gain leadership skills

Are you pregnant? We offer free services to help you and your new baby get ahead through the best health and education opportunities.

Who is it for?

Head Start is for families with kids from pregnancy to age 5. We provide individual learning plans for your child. We offer infant and toddler care, half-day programs, full-day programs, and home-based services.

You must meet certain guidelines in order to get into Head Start.

The first step is to look at how many people are in your home and how much money everyone earns.
